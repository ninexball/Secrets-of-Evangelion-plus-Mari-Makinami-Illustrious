=======================
Power of a name - Asuka
=======================
- We can see that Sohryu Asuka has great pride when she presents her name in classroom as Sohryu Asuka Langley in NGE Episode 8.
- Names are key to viewing others as distinct human beings deserving of due respect.
Sohryu Asuka Langley represents both an Eastern and western heritage name.
In Japan, people represent the name like: Sohryu Asuka or Langley Asuka
In Western, Asuka [Sohryu] Langley would be the common way of writing
However, Asuka writes it as Sohryu Asuka Langley, asserting herself and her ability to define herself irreverent of typical societal/cultural norms.
Asuka may reject that Sohryu and Shikinami are the same while she may accept that they have much in common.
